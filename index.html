About WebWizards

Webwizards are a small and agile group of computer science students, that specialize in web development and in applying new and innovative technologies. Our purpose is to reinforce the web profile of your company through the usage of functional and appealing web pages and other web services, resulting in a transition to the digital age. Some of the technologies we specialize in are:

  • Server Side: PHP//CakePHP, Python//Django, CGI
  • Client Side: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery
  • CMS: Joomla!, Wordpress, Django-CMS
