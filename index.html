About WebWizards

Webwizards are a small and agile group of computer science students, that specialize in web development and in applying new and innovative technologies. Some of the technologies we specialize in are:

  • Client Side: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery
  • CMS: Joomla!, Wordpress, Django-CMS
